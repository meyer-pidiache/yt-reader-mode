Immersive Reader Mode

Toggle between the normal video and an expanded AI chat panel synchronized with the player dimensions.

⚡

Auto-activate

Configure the extension to automatically enter Reader Mode on videos that feature AI capabilities.

💬

Initial Prompts

Set automatic text submissions so the AI starts your conversation immediately when you open a video.

⏱️

Smart Timestamps

Click on AI-suggested timestamps and automatically switch to video mode to watch the referenced segment.

How to use

  1. Go to YouTube — open any video with the AI chat panel ("Ask" / YouChat).
  2. Toggle Reader Mode — click the YT Reader Mode icon in the toolbar, or the toggle button that appears on the page.
  3. Customize — right-click the toolbar icon and select "Options" to adjust auto-activate and prompt settings.
